About Branden States

Hey, I’m Branden.

I’m a Junior student in the College of Journalism and Mass Communications at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln studying Sports Media and Communications, with minors in Statistics and Mathematics in an attempt to make my own sort of “path” for studying Sports Data Analytics. Sports have always been a huge part of my life, whether it was playing or watching them, and when I decided that Computer Engineering wasn’t going to be the career for me after one semester of classes, being able to apply my knowledge of code and computers in a field that actually interested me was too much to pass up. So, two years later, here we are, thanks to a series of great classes, I’m able to post my content on this website and hopefully get myself some form of media presence so that I can work for a franchise or organization in the future.

Hope you enjoy the little content I have presented, and thanks for checking out the blog.

-B
